ABOUT US

Who we are and what we do

EVERYDAY LUSAKA

Established 2018

Established in 2018, Everyday Lusaka is a photography platform and gallery that was developed to change conversations and perceptions of the city beyond the conventional photography found in Zambia.

OUR PRACTICE

Formats • Possibilities • Uses

By exploring different formats, possibilities and uses of photography – mobile, amateur, digital, print, written, collaborative, archival or exhibitory – we work to catalyse this shift towards a more considered visual representation of the country.
